## 2. Productized / subscription web agencies

**US/global:**
- **B12**: $42–49/mo DIY → $199–399/mo with human experts + $1,999 setup.
- **Designjoy-style subscriptions**: $199 → $699 (ManyPixels) → $1,500–2,950 (Teamtown) → $3,995–4,995/mo (Designjoy). Typical serious band: **$500–2,000/mo**. They sell *design capacity*, not a living website.

**Poland ("abonament na stronę www")** — established model, Givyx home turf:
- KlikAI (Orange partner): 39 zł net/mo (~$10) — telco budget end
- Najszybsza.pl: 29–59 zł/mo
- Typical market band: **100–150 zł net/mo** (~$25–38)
- Arena Stron: 249 zł/mo (~$62) with tech support
- Rimset: 329 zł/mo (~$82)
- Strona Biznesu: 369 zł net/mo
- eSpark: 399 zł net/mo (~$100), 12-mo commit, 15 subpages, WordPress, maintenance + **monthly analytics report**; **client owns site after 12 months**; ongoing care 199 zł/mo after
- Standard inclusions: build + hosting + SSL + small content changes + basic SEO. Cancel-anytime and "own after 12 months" are common trust levers.

**Slovakia:**
- NaŠtartujWeb.sk: from **€30/mo + €200 setup** — hosting, security, backups, adjustments, SEO; free demo + book-a-call CTAs
- BiznisWeb.sk: monthly/annual e-shop packages, no setup fee
- Market: custom agency site €1,500–2,500 one-off; maintenance €30–150/mo

**Takeaway:** In PL/SK "website as subscription" is normalized at **€25–100/mo**, but delivered by humans, slow, AI-free. **Nobody in CEE is doing it AI-native.**

## 3. What converts visitors (top players' tactics)

1. **Instant AI preview before commitment** — the dominant pattern. Durable "30 seconds from 3 questions"; B12 "draft in 60 seconds from name + category". The product demo IS the lead magnet → endowment effect → paywall to keep it.
2. **$0 entry + free subdomain** — upgrade wall = custom domain + payments + branding removal.
3. **Risk reversal** — 30-day money-back (Durable, Hostinger, 10Web), 14-day no-card trials, "cancel anytime" everywhere.
4. **Price anchoring & bundling** — strikethrough discounts, "free domain worth $30", "$5,000+ saved vs agency", B12's "$1/mo for 3 months".
5. **Free adjacent tools as SEO lead magnets** — business-name generators, logo makers, invoice generators.
6. **Social proof numerics** — "3,000,000+ businesses", Trustpilot scores, template galleries as output proof.

## 4. Top 5 table-stakes (a new entrant must have)

1. **Instant, free, minimal-signup AI site preview** (<60s) — the universal conversion mechanic.
2. **Free tier on subdomain + free custom domain (yr 1) on paid**, SSL/hosting bundled invisibly.
3. **Risk reversal on the pricing page**: 14–30 day money-back + cancel anytime.
4. **Core SMB features in base paid plan**: responsive site, basic SEO, analytics, forms, booking, simple payments/ecommerce (competitors include ecommerce even on entry tiers).
5. **AI editing after launch via chat** — post-launch conversational edits are now expected; metered credits are the accepted mechanism.

## 5. Top 5 differentiation opportunities for Givyx

1. **"AI keeps your site alive" — own the maintenance gap.** Builders convert on *creation*; none sells ongoing autonomous maintenance. B12 does it with expensive humans ($199–399/mo + $1,999 setup). Position as **"B12 outcomes at Durable prices"**: an AI account manager that proactively ships monthly improvements, with a visible **"what your AI did this month" report** (retention engine, mirrors eSpark's report ritual).
2. **CEE-native, truly multilingual (EN/PL/SK/RU).** Global builders are English-first; PL/SK abonament agencies are human/slow/AI-free. Nobody offers AI generation + maintenance in Polish/Slovak with local invoicing (faktura VAT), local payments (Przelewy24/BLIK, GoPay), zł/€ pricing, RODO/GDPR-correct legal pages auto-generated.
3. **Chat as the entire lifecycle interface (MCP moat).** Owner texts "add a Christmas promo banner, close bookings Dec 24–26" → it happens. No self-serve builder offers this for the whole lifecycle.
4. **Outcome-priced flat plans, not credits/tokens.** Credit anxiety is the #1 complaint about Lovable/Bolt/10Web. Flat "your site stays perfect" plans = agency reliability at SaaS price; matches how PL/SK SMBs already buy.
5. **Ownership + trust levers from CEE agencies**: "own/export your site" promise, free demo + human call available, optional human review on higher tiers, no setup fee (vs B12 $1,999, SK €200).

## 6. Pricing recommendation

Reference: self-serve AI builders $10–25 entry / $25–50 growth / $99+ premium; PL abonament 100–400 zł/mo; SK €30–100/mo; B12 proves $199–399/mo payable for "someone else maintains it."

Suggested ladder:
- **Free / Preview** — $0: AI site on givyx subdomain, limited AI edits. Pure funnel.
- **Launch** — **€19–29/mo (~89–129 zł, ~$25)**: custom domain (free yr 1), hosting, SSL, booking, forms, basic SEO, monthly AI edit allowance. Undercuts Wix Core/Squarespace Core; low end of PL abonament band.
- **Grow (flagship)** — **€49–69/mo (~229–299 zł, ~$55–75)**: + storefront/payments, proactive AI maintenance (monthly content/SEO refresh, "what AI did" report), multilingual versions, priority chat. The "AI keeps it living" tier — above Durable Grow, far below B12 Professional.
- **Studio / Pro** — **€129–199/mo (~$140–215)**: multi-location/multi-site, advanced ecommerce, human review, SLA. Captures local-agency and B12 buyers.
- **No setup fee** (differentiator); optional paid "concierge migration."
- **Mechanics:** annual = ~2 months free, 30-day money-back, cancel anytime, own/export promise, **local-currency display (zł/€)** — itself a conversion lever in CEE.
