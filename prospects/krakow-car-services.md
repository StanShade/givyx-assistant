## Patterns (sales intelligence)
- **Nowa Huta = most underserved district** (~2/3 of workshops have no site); Mistrzejowice,
  Wzgórza Krzesławickie similar. Bronowice/Krowodrza mostly covered.
- **`*.krakow.pl` broken-SSL cluster is a goldmine**: 25–30-year-old firms with dead HTTPS —
  the browser warning is a verifiable, non-insulting opener.
- **Free-subdomain shops (dobrywarsztat.info) already pay for online tools** — proven willingness
  to invest; ideal subscription customers (Intra Cars, All Cars, M-TRAK).
- **Free-mail addresses everywhere** (op.pl/interia/onet/wp) → own-domain email is a sellable
  bundle item.
- All data = business contact info from public business directories.
